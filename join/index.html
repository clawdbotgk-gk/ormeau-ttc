Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common membership enquiries.

Can I book a table without joining? expand_more
Currently, we are at full capacity and do not offer casual table bookings for non-members. Participation is through our structured membership programs and Saturday trial sessions only.
What is the minimum age for Juniors? expand_more
Our Junior academy accepts players from age 6 and up. We offer coaching pathways for all ability levels within the youth section.
Is there a long-term contract? expand_more
No, our memberships are rolling monthly subscriptions. We require 30 days notice for cancellation, but there is no long-term tie-in.
Do I need my own equipment for the trial? expand_more
While we recommend players eventually purchase their own bat, we can provide equipment for your first few sessions. Just wear sports clothing and indoor trainers.
